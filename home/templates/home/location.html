Location

Schloßkulm is located on a hill between Saalfeld and Rudolstadt in Central-East Germany amongst orchards and mixed forests. The term "Schloß" in "Schloßkulm" translates to castle. You will not find a castle here anymore but there are still 14 half-timbered houses and a central bakehouse left standing. About 50 inhabitants live and farm in our village and surrounding area. There are good transport connections to nearby cities Erfurt, Jena and Weimar. The Thuringian forest is close by and leads through dreamlike landscapes.
