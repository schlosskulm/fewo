Lower Apartment

Dear guests,

welcome to Schloßkulm! Located just at the edge of the forest you can expect a beautiful location full of wild flowers, singing birds, the sound of wind in the trees and nice, welcoming hosts. In the cold months you will have to operate the oven (it is not possible without!) and in summer you can count the stars in the sky. Even though only the foundation of this extended barn tells the story of Schloßkulm it is those old bricks that make this vacational place unique.

It is worthwhile to even stay for a longer time. The internet is quite stable, there is almost no distraction but a lot of opportunities to make good use of breaks. This is an ideal place to work on your projects or for strategy meetings, as well as recreative familiy trips. You will find a simple and cosy home on two floors.

In the house you can expect a minimalist and rustic configuration: a lot of wood and adobe, a wood oven that needs to be operated in the cold months (the water and heating system is connected to it - there will always be wood provided for you) and original furniture. It is an old house that only has been renovated as to the most important points. You are equipped with most items for bigger cooking adventures - if something is missing you are welcome to approach us. On agreement you can also light a fire and/or do a BBQ in the garden after rainfall (otherwise there is too much of a fire hazard).

On the basement floor you will find a simple and cozy home with two rooms and a bathroom. The bed in the hallway room is 2 x 1,60m and the one in the living room / kitchen room 2 x 1,80m. Bed linen and towels are provided.

The garden is perfect for families with kids as you do not have to cross any street to reach the forest and outdoors. Two old wendy houses can be found in the garden - my own children played here when they were younger :) Now they are waiting for new input from your kids ;)

The toilet house is fully functional. You are welcome to bring your dog(s).

The lower apartment caters to max. 4 guests.

  • High preventive fire protection rules
  • Smoking inside the house is not allowed
  • Campfires and BBQs in the garden are not allowed
  • Inside the house you are allowed max. 1 candle or tea light on a fireproof surface and only under your supervision
  • Do not place any inflammable items on top of or above the oven, and nothing in range of 1m in front of it
  • Please pay attention to the local fire threat level
